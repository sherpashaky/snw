Directions to Astoria World Manor!


Conveniently located in the heart of Astoria, Queens

FROM BROOKLYN, QUEENS & LONG ISLAND
TAKE ANY EXPRESSWAY OR PARKWAY ONTO GRAND CENTRAL PARKWAY GOING TOWARDS RFK BRIDGE. TAKE EXIT 45 TOWARDS 32ST STREET (LAST EXIT BEFORE THE BRIDGE). PROCEED STRAIGHT ON ASTORIA BLVD/HOYT AVE. TURN LEFT ONTO CRESCENT ST, THEN TURN LEFT ONTO ASTORIA BLVD. ASTORIA WORLD MANOR IS LOCATED AT 25-32 ASTORIA BLVD, ON THE RIGHT.
FROM MANHATTAN
TAKE THE QUEENSBOROUGH BRIDGE (UPPER LEVEL, KEEP RIGHT). TAKE THE RAMP TO 21ST ST. STAY ON 21ST ST FOR 1.8 MILES, THEN TURN RIGHT ONTO ASTORIA BLVD. ASTORIA WORLD MANOR IS AT 25-32 ASTORIA BLVD.
SUBWAY
THE N or Q TRAIN LINE TO ASTORIA BLVD STATION
